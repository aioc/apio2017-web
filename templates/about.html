About

The Asia-Pacific Informatics Olympiad (APIO) is an IOI-like competition for delegations within the South Asian / Western Pacific region. The contest will be held online, with students competing at contest sites within their own country or area.

APIO 2017 is organized by the Australian Informatics Olypiad Committee, and will be held on Saturday, May 13th, 2017. The first competition was organized by the Australian IOI team in 2007.
